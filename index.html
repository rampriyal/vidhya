Information on spotted wilt virus of tomato

Spotted wilt virus also known as tomato spotted wilt virus causes symptoms in leaf such as necrotic spots with chlorotic rings.Wilting is particularly common and also leads to bronzing of leaves.Same symptoms appear in fruit and in severe cases leads to stunting of young seedlings.

Vector of tomato spotted wilt virus

The virus is primarily transmitted by thrips to healthy plants while feeding

Management

  • cultural methods - field sanitation,isolation and crop rotation
  • biological methods- use of predators like pirate bugs
  • chemical methods-insecticides targeting thrips

Chemicals used with recommended dose

chemicals recommended dose
Spinosad 1.5-3ml/liter
Imidacloprid 0.25-0.5ml/liter
Azadirachtin 1-3ml/liter
Thiamethoxam 0.3-0.6g/liter

Resistant varieties

  • Red defender
  • Crista
  • BHN444
  • southern star
  • quincy
